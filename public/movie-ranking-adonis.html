The Best Movies According to Adonis

My top 3 movies of all-time

Pulp Fiction

It's a violent movie, with a lot of good actors, directed by Tarantino and with a lot of spectacular dialogues and a lot of funny scenes.

Duna

One of the best distopic stories of all times and incredible actors that shows a story of a guy (Paul) and his journey to take back everything that was stolen from his family and people.

Ex Machina

Really cool sci-fi movie that makes us think about what makes us human and if machines can be like us.
